Nag Tibba Trek

Rs.1,800 / Per person

Overview

One of the most popular treks near Dehradun, Nag Tibba (‘Serpent’s Peak’) standing tall at 3020m. is an ideal weekend trek suited for most people looking for a refresher from the boring routine. Nag Tibba Trek can be done throughout the winter season when most of the treks in higher Himalayan ranges are closed. Nag Tibba from Delhi is only 3 days hike which makes it a perfect winter hike nearby. Given its easy level of challenge, anyone with normal fitness can do this trek. You will get a glimpse of the famous Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, Srikanth, Black peak, and many more peaks from Nag Tibba. The Nag Tibba range offers some of the best camping locations. The route goes through thick deodar forest and with only a day into the trek, one reaches to a very remote region with almost no habitation. A great refresher and trailer for new trekkers who are yet to see the full movie of a long trek. The total Nag Tibba trek distance is 19 km. The best months to do Nag Tibba Trek for snow are December to March as the trails are completely snow-laden during these months. On Naag Tibba, the temperature at times goes as low as -7 degrees.     Trek Itinerary

    01

    Day 1: Drive to Pantwari & Trek to Nag Mandir Campsite (5.5 kms)

    Reporting time at Dehradun bus stand is 07:00 AM. We will drive to Pantwari from there. We will reach Pantwari at 10 AM (Approx). Nag Tibba Trek starts from here. Freshening up & breakfast will be in a hotel on the way to Pantwari. From Pantwari start trekking to the base of Nag Tibba summit with packed lunch. The trek is uphill & will take 4 to 5 Hrs. We will stop en route for lunch. After reaching the base by the late afternoon we will set up camps. Explore the surrounding area, take pictures, and have loads of fun. Be ready for the cold night, as the temperature could get negative.

    02

    Day 2: Nag Mandir - Nag Tibba Summit & Return to Nag Mandir (5 kms)

    Wake up to the sound of chirping birds. Enjoy the morning views of first light falling on the high mountain peaks all around. After Breakfast head for Nag Tibba Summit with packed lunch. From Naag Tibba enjoy the views of the snow clad mountain ranges of Garhwal and Himachal. You will get to see the famous Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, Srikanth, Black peak and many more peaks from here. Spend some time enjoying the view. We have our packed lunch here. After lunch, we head back to Nag Mandir by the same route.

    03

    Day 3: Nag Mandir to Pantwari (5.5 kms). Depart from Pantwari.

    We wake up early in the morning. After warm-up & breakfast, we head back on the trail leading to Pantwari. The trail is mostly downhill with only a few sections of uphill. We are expected to arrive in Pantwari by 2-3 PM calling an end to the Nag Tibba winter trek. From here we board the vehicle for Dehradun. We are expected to arrive in Dehradun by 9 PM. If you are booking your Dehradun- Delhi transport on the same night, you are advised to book it around 11 PM.
